QUALITY WEEK CELEBRATION

Every year, the second Thursday of November is celebrated as World Quality Day to create worldwide awareness around ‘Quality Culture’ and emphasize the importance of quality for organizational growth. Companies everywhere plan engaging events to commemorate the occasion.

This year, the theme for World Quality Day was ‘Creating Customer Value’.

To commemorate the occasion, we organized a ‘Quality Week’between 9th to 12th Nov 2020, wherein multiple engaging,fun-filled events highlighting the theme were held virtually.We encouraged our colleagues to participate and share their ideas/experiences around quality and creating customer values. Thanks to all the COBRA team members who helped transform this celebration into a learning opportunity, wherein, everyone came together to brainstorm, gain knowledge, cross-link, and convert thoughts into reality.

Message from Timo Franz, Head of Business Excellence (SMO RI BE): Quality is about meeting customer needs. Steve Jobs described that as a tricky challenge as “You can’t just ask customers what they want and then try to give that to them. By the time you get it built, they’ll want something new.” W.E. Deming (A well-known Quality Scholar) would have agreed and added: “The moral is that it is necessary to innovate, to predict needs of the customer, give him more. He that innovates and is lucky will take the market.” True for internal customers as well.

Timo Franz,
Head of Business Excellence (SMO RI BE)
