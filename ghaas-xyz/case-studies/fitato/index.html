The Story

Fitato was started by two college friends with a dream to revolutionise the fitness industry.

With $100K in seed funding, they created a technology platform that enables a user to access any gym or fitness studio across the town. They partnered with 100 gyms and fitness studios in Pune. Things were going according to plan until they went out and started selling their Fitato memberships.

Fitato eventually reached out to us and engaged us for building their growth story.

The Problem

Fitato is a high growth startup. The challenge was to build the processes that would script the growth story.

We began our engagement by running some experiments to clearly identify what the problems were.
