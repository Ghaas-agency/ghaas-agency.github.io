What is Fitato for Workplace?

Fitato is the largest fitness network that offers universal membership to 300+ best gyms and fitness centers around the Pune city and on-demand fitness classes at your workplace. One pass for every employee fitness need. Workout anytime, anywhere, in a facility next to your office or home.

Shape a culture of fitness in your team with access to premium fitness classes and the tools to increase motivation and connection.

For Employees

The biggest workout blockers? Convenience, lack of motivation and most importantly lack of flexibility. Fitato’s flexible fitness membership makes working out actually work out for your team.

The network comprises the best gyms and fitness studios in the city. Whether your team wants to go to the gym, yoga class or de-stress with a swimming session or pump up with a boxing class, you can go to the same facility every day or try a new one.

Fitato is the perfect solution and fitness is right around the corner. All with a simple aim enabling your team that hour a day where they connect with their mind, body, and soul.

For Employers

The benefits of working out extend far beyond the gym: studies show it improves concentration, enhances creativity and lowers stress. Partner with Fitato and give your employees access to the classes they need to stay motivated personally and professionally.

Better yet? Your employees can work out together to build their strength and their sense of team. Plus, all corporate clients receive an onsite kickoff, custom landing pages, communication and templates, monthly reports and dedicated account management support to ensure your employees stay engaged.
